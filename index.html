About Sunset Paddle Co.

At Sunset Paddle Co., we believe the best moments happen on the water—especially when the sun begins to kiss the horizon. Based in the heart of Rehoboth Beach, we offer thoughtfully curated paddleboard and kayak rentals for locals and visitors looking to experience Delaware's coast from a new perspective.

Whether you're a seasoned paddler or trying it for the first time, our team is here to make your journey effortless, safe, and unforgettable. We specialize in golden hour adventures—those calm, magical times of day when the bay glows with color and the world feels still.

We're more than just a rental shop—we're a locally rooted experience. As a family-owned business, we pride ourselves on personal service, community connection, and helping people unplug and reconnect with nature.

Our Services

Paddleboard Rentals

Explore the calm waters of Rehoboth Bay on one of our premium paddleboards. Perfect for all skill levels, our boards come with all necessary safety equipment and a brief orientation.

Kayak Rentals

Single or tandem kayaks available for those who prefer to sit back and enjoy the scenery. Great for couples or families wanting to explore together with included life jackets.

Sunset Tours

Join our guided sunset tours where we'll take you to the best spots to watch the sky transform into a masterpiece of colors over the Delaware coast.
